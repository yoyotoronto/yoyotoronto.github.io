Where to buy yo-yos?

Disclaimer: YoYoToronto does not provide customer support for any retailers listed below.

Online retailers

For the biggest and latest selections, we buy our yo-yos online. Our members have purchased from these retailers and found them reliable:

  • 44 Emporium
  • YoYoExpert
  • YoYoSam
  • Yo-Yo Store REWIND

When making a purchase online, make sure to read the retailer's policies carefully (eg. currency, shipping, payment methods, etc)

Local stores in Toronto

  • Treasure Island Toys near Pape and Danforth (Map)

From other club members

Occasionally some of our members would sell off their old yo-yos by posting ads in our Facebook group. On the contrary, potential buyers can also post requests to look for certain yo-yos. (Note that you must live in the Greater Toronto Area to make posts to buy or sell)

Keep in mind that used yoyos can vary in conditions and prices. It's a good idea to look up how much they originally cost at one of the retailers above, and decide whether a deal is good.
