Just getting started?

Great! Check out YoYoExpert.com and YoYoTricks.com. You will find video instructions on the basics of yo-yos: from putting the yo-yo on your hand and winding the string, to throwing properly and learning some of your first tricks.

After the basics, what's next?

If you look around youtube, there is no shortage of advanced yo-yo trick tutorials, many of which are developed by very creative people. The Canadian Yo-yo Association has a compilation of some of the best ones out there.

How do I stay updated?

Make sure you check the Canadian Yo-yo Association (CANYA)'s website often. They provide resources to connect you to other yo-yo clubs, contests, and events happening in Canada and around the world.
